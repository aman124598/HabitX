## About

Habit X is a minimalist habit tracker designed to help you stay consistent with your daily goals. Track your habits, build streaks, and watch your progress grow over time.

- **Simple Habit Tracking** - Create and track daily habits with one tap
- **Streak Counter** - Build momentum with streak tracking
- **Progress Stats** - View your completion rates and trends
- **Dark Mode** - Easy on the eyes, day or night
- **Notifications** - Get reminded to complete your habits
- **Cloud Sync** - Your data syncs across devices
- **Offline Support** - Track habits even without internet
