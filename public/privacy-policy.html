Habit X — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-12-31

Habit X ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This page explains what data we collect, why we collect it, how it is used, and how you can manage or request deletion of your data.

Data we collect

  • Push notification tokens (Expo/FCM tokens) — collected when you enable push notifications; used to deliver notifications and reminders. Tokens are sent to push providers (Expo or Firebase Cloud Messaging) and our backend servers.
  • Account information (email, username) — provided directly by you during registration; used for account authentication and management.
  • Habit and progress data — your habits, completions, streaks, and gamification progress are stored to provide the core functionality of the app.
  • Crash reports and diagnostics — collected automatically to improve app stability and performance.

How we use and share data

Push tokens are shared with push providers as needed to deliver notifications. Account and habit data is stored on our servers and is not sold to third parties. We use analytics data to improve the app experience.

Security and retention

Data transmitted by the app is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS). Account data is retained until you request deletion or delete your account. Habit and progress data is retained as long as your account is active.

User controls

To opt out of personalized advertising, you may reset or limit your device Advertising ID using your device settings. You can disable push notifications in-app or via your device settings. To request deletion of your account or personal data, contact us at support@aman1298.com.

For more details or to submit requests, contact support@aman1298.com.
